How do I care for my flowers?

We don't compromise on freshness and quality. Your flowers go through extensive quality controls before eventually arriving in your hands. However, flower care is very important to help maximize the beauty and longevity of your blooms, allowing them to reach their full potential.

If you follow these simple steps, your flowers will last as long as possible!

1. Give your flowers a quick snip before putting them in a vase. This allows the stems to properly hydrate.

2. Fill a clean vase with cool water and stir in some of the provided flower food.

3. Remove any leaves that would be below the waterline and place your flowers in the vase.

4. Change the water daily adding some more flower food each time. Trim the stems every few days.

5. Keep you flowers in a cool dry location and remove stems as they expire to keep the others blooming bright.
